FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about solar energy? We've got answers. Find out how Luxsun Energy India can help you make the switch with confidence.

We offer On-Grid (utility-interactive), Off-Grid (independent battery backup), and Hybrid solar systems. Each configuration is tailored to match residential, commercial, or agricultural needs, maximizing cost benefits and efficiency.

Our engineering team performs a structural assessment and energy load audit. Depending on whether you have a stable power grid (ideal for On-Grid setups) or regular blackouts (ideal for Off-Grid or Hybrid with battery back-up), we customize the panels and inverters accordingly.

For most residential and commercial installations, the break-even period ranges between 3 to 5 years. Given that panels have a lifetime of 25+ years, you will enjoy free electricity for the remaining 20+ years.

Solar energy systems in India typically yield an ROI of 20% to 30% per annum. This outperforms traditional investments like bank FDs, mutual funds, or real estate, while simultaneously providing high-value property appreciation.

Customers can save up to 80% to 90% on their electricity bills. In many instances, during peak solar generation seasons, excess power is exported back to the grid (via net metering), resulting in credits and minimal utility expenses.

Solar panels require minimal maintenance. Periodic cleaning (wiping down dirt, dust, and foliage) ensures optimum sunlight absorption. Keeping the panels clean preserves their output efficiency and secures long-term ROI yields.

Yes, the Government of India provides attractive subsidies for residential rooftop solar systems under PM Surya Ghar Yojana. Additionally, we assist customers in securing low-cost financing options and bank loans for hassle-free solar investments.

We partner with leading financial institutions to provide flexible EMI schemes, low-interest solar project loans, and direct installment options. Our team manages the paperwork to ensure quick approvals and subsidy collection.
