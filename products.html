Manufacturing Partner: WAAREE

Waaree Energies Limited was founded in 1989, with headquarters in Mumbai, India. Waaree has India's largest aggregate installed capacity of 12 GW as of June 30, 2023, at its plants in Chikhli, Surat, Tumb, and Nandigram in Gujarat and is a top player in the solar module export for Fiscal 2023. The company has supplied more than 11 GW of solar modules and commissioned more than 1.1 GW solar EPC projects.

Types of Solar Systems

A Comprehensive Guide to different solar configurations and their individual benefits.

On-Grid Solar

Connects directly to the public utility grid. Ideal for residential households looking to reduce monthly electricity costs using net metering benefits.

Off-Grid Solar

Runs independently with a dedicated battery storage bank. Perfect for remote structures, farmhouses, and areas with frequent grid blackouts.

Hybrid Solar

Combines grid connection with smart battery storage backup. Ensures seamless electrical power during blackouts while offering net metering savings.
